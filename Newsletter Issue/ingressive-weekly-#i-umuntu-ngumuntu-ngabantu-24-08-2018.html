Hardcore Developer

{Hard Developer! Hard Developer!! but you can't code without using frameworks}

Recently, a couple of opportunities has been popping up on the radar, with a call for developers to build stuff without the use of frameworks and whatnot which is breathtakingly insane if you ask me.
This supports the opinion of Neo Ighodaro (@NeoIghodaro) CTO, hotels.ng who tweeted a time ago that developers should learn the core fundamentals of their languages or technology stack before fully immersing themselves in their accompanying frameworks.
An example of that is the JS13Kgames challenge, which you are to build a functional game in Vanilla JavaScript {no I do not mean Icecream, I mean pure bare-bones JavaScript}that is no bigger than 13 kilobytes with no framework and other specifications.
